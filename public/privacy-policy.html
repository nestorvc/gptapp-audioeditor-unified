Privacy Policy

Audio Console ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our audio editing service, including when accessed through ChatGPT or other platforms.

Information We Collect

Audio Files

When you use Audio Console, you may upload audio files or provide URLs to audio files for processing. We store these files solely for processing purposes to perform the requested operations (trimming, format conversion, vocal separation, etc.). Processed and exported audio files are stored temporarily on Amazon Web Services (AWS) S3 cloud storage and are automatically deleted after 24 hours.

Usage Information

We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve our service, including:

  • Device information (device type, screen dimensions)
  • Browser and operating system information
  • Language preferences
  • Session identifiers (anonymous, not linked to personal identity)
  • Audio processing metadata (format, duration, file size - but not audio content)
  • Feature usage patterns (which tools are used, processing times)

What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Audio content or actual audio data in our analytics
  • File names that could identify individuals (file names are sanitized)
  • Location data
  • Payment information (our service is free to use)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your audio files according to your requests (trimming, conversion, vocal separation, etc.)
  • Store processed audio files temporarily on AWS S3 to provide download links
  • Analyze service usage patterns to improve functionality and performance
  • Debug technical issues and ensure service reliability
  • Understand which features are most popular to guide future development

Third-Party Services

AWS S3

Processed audio files are stored on Amazon Web Services S3. Files are stored temporarily and are subject to AWS's privacy policy and security measures. We use AWS S3 to provide you with download links for your processed audio files.

LALAL.AI

When you use vocal separation features, your audio files are processed by LALAL.AI's service. This is necessary to provide vocal/music separation functionality. LALAL.AI's processing is subject to their own privacy policy and terms of service.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to track anonymous usage statistics. This helps us understand how users interact with our service. No personally identifiable information is sent to Google Analytics. All tracking is done server-side and complies with privacy regulations.

ChatGPT/OpenAI

When you access Audio Console through ChatGPT, your interactions are also subject to OpenAI's privacy policy and terms of service.

Data Storage and Retention

Audio files are stored only for processing purposes and are subject to automatic deletion:

  • Uploaded source files are processed and then immediately deleted from our servers
  • All processed and exported audio files are stored temporarily on AWS S3
  • All processed and exported audio files are automatically deleted after 24 hours from the time of processing
  • We do not retain audio files beyond the 24-hour period required for processing and download
  • Analytics data (metadata only, not audio content) is stored by Google Analytics according to their retention policies

Important: You are responsible for downloading and saving any processed files you wish to keep before the 24-hour deletion period expires. We cannot recover files after they have been automatically deleted.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure file uploads using encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Files stored on AWS S3 with appropriate access controls
  • Server-side processing with secure temporary file handling
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can request information about what data we have collected
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your audio files (note: files are automatically deleted after processing)
  • Opt-out: While we don't collect PII, you can disable analytics tracking if desired

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

Children's Privacy

Audio Console is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. By using Audio Console, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
